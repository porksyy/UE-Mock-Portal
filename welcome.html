Welcome to the UE Online Application for Admission!

To proceed with your application, please do the following:

  1. Be ready to upload your 2" x 2" photo with white background and any of the following
    • For Grade 12 Graduates: Grade 12 Report Card
    • For Graduates and Non-Formal Education: PEPT/ALS Rating
    • For Current Grade 12 Students: Grade 11 Report Card
    • For College Transferees: Transcript of Records (ToR) or Certification of Grades
    • For Degree Holders: Transcript of Records (ToR)
    • For Cross-Enrollees: Permit to cross-enroll
  2. Review all of the information that you entered in the application form before clicking the 'Submit' button.
  3. Make sure to COPY/TAKE A SCREENSHOT of your reference number. You will use this later on.
